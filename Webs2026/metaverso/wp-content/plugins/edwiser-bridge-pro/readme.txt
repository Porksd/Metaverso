==  Installation  ==

= Minimum Requirements =
* PHP version 7.0 or greater
* WordPress 6.0 or higher
* Moodle 4.3 or higher

= Automatic Installation =
* Upon purchasing the Edwiser Bridge PRO, an email  containing the purchase receipt, download link and license key will be sent to your registered email id. Copy the license key from this email.
* Login to your WordPress site as an administrator.
* Go to Edwiser Bridge settings page from the dashboard.
* Click on the ‘Licenses’ tab on this page.
* Enter the license key and click on the ‘Install Plugin’ button.
* Plugin will be installed automatically and activated.

=  Manual Installation  =
* Upon purchasing the Edwiser Bridge PRO, an email  containing the purchase receipt, download link and license key will be sent to your registered email id. You can download the ‘edwiser-bridge-pro.zip’ extension file using the download link provided.
* To install this plugin, go to the ‘Plugins’ menu from the dashboard.
* Click on the ‘Add New’ button on this page.
* Now click on the ‘Upload Plugin‘ button and upload the ‘edwiser-bridge-pro.zip’ file.
* Click on ‘Install Now’ button once you have located and uploaded the plugin.
* On successful installation click the ‘Activate Plugin’ link to activate the plugin.
* Alternatively, you can unzip and upload the Edwiser Bridge PRO plugin folder using the FTP application of your choice.
